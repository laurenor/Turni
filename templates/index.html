About

Turni helps tournament organizers make maps based on brackets data from Challonge. Participants are given the option to receive texts on their phone with the location of where they should be reporting, when it's their turn to play.

Features

    For Tournament Organizers

  • >> Create interactive and customizable venue maps in seconds from Challonge brackets.

  • >> Nice to have*: Create a general floor plan for participants that comes equipped with a chat box and an optional Twitch stream.

    • For participants

  • >> Get texts when it's your turn to play.

  • >> Gain access to a venue map provided by your TO, so you know where to go.
